Why Traditional SSE Breaks in an AI World

Secure Service Edge (SSE) was designed for a simpler reality.

A world where:

  • A human initiates an action
  • Against a known application
  • Over a predictable request/response flow

That model worked ‚Äî until AI changed the actor, the data flow, and the risk model entirely.

AI didn't just introduce a new app category ‚Äî it introduced new actors.

Today, we see:

  • Copilots embedded inside apps
  • Autonomous AI agents acting on behalf of users
  • Model Context Protocol (MCP) connections pulling from internal datasets
  • Chained tools executing decisions without human confirmation
Key Insight: The "user" is no longer the decision-maker. The "app" is no longer the boundary. And the outcome is no longer deterministic.
SSE was built to protect traffic. AI creates behavior.
