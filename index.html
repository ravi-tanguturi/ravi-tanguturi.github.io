I write about enterprise security architecture at the intersection of AI, data, and scale — where technical decisions directly shape business risk, resilience, and velocity.

Over the past 15+ years, I’ve partnered with CISOs, security architects, and executive leadership teams across Fortune 500 organizations to design and operationalize security architectures that protect sensitive data, meet regulatory obligations, and enable innovation at enterprise scale. My work spans AI Security, Zero Trust, Secure Service Edge (SSE), data protection, and governance frameworks across complex, globally distributed environments.

This site explores how security leaders must move beyond traditional perimeter and control-based models toward AI-ready architectures — integrating DSPM, continuous governance, and real-time guardrails that align security outcomes with business priorities. The goal is not security for its own sake, but resilient systems that allow organizations to adopt AI with confidence, control, and clarity.
